Welcome to Milton's Pages.

About Me: I'm currently master student in computer science major. My research direction focus on visual SLAM, 3D reconstruction and semantic segmentation. The combination of these domains leads to semantic 3D reconstruction, semantic localization and mapping. My goal is to build such a computer visual system to understand physical environment. The system should not only be able to interpret the 3D geometrical structure of environment, but also capture the semantic information for it (e.g. a street-view scene is composed of pedestrians, cars, trees, pavements, sky, poles).Therefore, our main goal in this research project is to build a visual perception system which integrates 3D geometrical and semantic information to understand the scene deeply.
