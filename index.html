Welcome to Milton's Pages.

Hi, I'm (Milton)Chi Chong Wong. I'm currently a PhD student in computer science major from University of Macau (UM). My research direction focus on 3D computer vision and its application in 3D shape analysis and intelligent transportation system. My current reseach projects are: i) 3D data (point cloud / mesh) processing and analysis ; ii) outdoor scene semantic understanding from 2D images and 3D point clouds ; iii) reliable vehicular pedestrian detection.
